The Bottleneck

The painting industry runs on precision, but the way we estimate is broken. Manual takeoffs are a massive bottleneck for commercial contractors. Every hour spent counting doors is an hour not spent reviewing scope or winning jobs.

01 // Plan Reading
Clicking through hundreds of pages, manually scaling and measuring walls, ceilings, and trim.
02 // Spec Cross-Reference
Flipping between drawing notes and the Division 09 spec book to figure out the right system.
03 // Spreadsheet Gymnastics
Reconciling counts across drawings, rolling up areas by system, padding for waste, and hoping the formulas didn’t drift.
04 // Bid Package Assembly
Formatting proposals and assembling submittals at 11 PM the night before the bid is due.

Built for Commercial. Built Tough.

Plan Reading That Doesn’t Hallucinate

Reads PDFs like an experienced estimator. Identifies paintable surfaces, calculates square footage from drawings, reads the spec book for product requirements.

Commercial Paint Knowledge Built-In

Calibrated to Real Job Data

Built on real historical job data. Knows labor rates, crew productivity by surface type, material coverage rates, and regional pricing.
